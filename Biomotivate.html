BIOMOTIVATE

Biomotivate aims to decrease the relapse rate for populations recovering from opioid-use addiction - an epidemic especially prevalent in the greater Pittsburgh area. Using wearable technology, the goal is to help addicts become more self-aware physiologically in hopes that they will seek help before they relapse.


I worked on multiple projects as part of the Design and Development Team - the PDF and staff dashboard project. I interviewed recovering opioid-use addicts from a number of rehabilitation centers around the pittsburgh area. Using the feedback I got from each visit, I iterated over the original designs. I created storyboards for the staff dashboard and brainstormed questions to ask staff members about the effectiveness of having a dashboard.

Unfortunately, I am unable to publicly disclose a lot of information regarding the research I contributed to, as Biomotivate is still in the process of developing a lot of their main products. However, if you're interested, please reach out :)
