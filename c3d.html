C3D.io

Codeca is an ed tech company that offers programming resources and products aimed at helping teachers without much coding experience. I was in charge of redesigning the website for one of their main products: C3D.io - a web based VR programming platform for students. I began by modifying their entire site map and from there redesigned every page along with some design assets. I've chosen some of the main pages to highlight.

PROBLEM

The current site is hard to navigate because information is not organized or grouped properly.

BUSINESS OBJECTIVES

01 Increase conversion rate

02 Improve SEO performance

SITE MAP

Thinking more deeply about the information hierarchy, I decided to start with creating a new site map. One of the biggest issues they wanted to address with the redesigned website was the low conversion rate. Customers seemed to get confused navigating their old site and most likely left to a competitor page.

TEACHERS PAGE

The teachers page was the most critical page I had to design for the website because the goal of this page is to sell their primary product - the school license. We wanted teachers to 1) find the page and 2) purchase the license.

TOP PAGE

PARENTS PAGE

MAINTAINING CONSISTENCY

Multiple different designers had worked on the website before me causing inconsistencies between the different pages. Additionally, the style appeared outdated, which did not align with the forward-thinking vision of the company. I refined their style guide and redesigned many sections and design assets to be better aligned with the company's vision.
